FAQ
Questions
answered.
Can I use GoClip for someone else's channel? +
Yes — GoClip works with any public Twitch channel. Streamers can clip their own VODs, and clippers can run it on their favorite streamer's channel. Just enter the channel name and you're good to go.
How does the AI decide which clips to keep? +
GoClip transcribes the VOD using Whisper, then sends the full transcript to Claude AI. Claude scores each moment based on humor, hype, reactions, and viral potential. You set the minimum quality score — only clips that hit your threshold get rendered.
Do I need to keep my PC on? +
Yes — GoClip is a Windows desktop app. Keep it running and it handles everything automatically. You can minimize it to the taskbar and forget about it.
What does the watermark on the free tier look like? +
Free tier clips include a small GoClip watermark on the video. It's removed completely on Pro and Premium plans. Upgrading also removes the 3 clip/week limit.
What do I need to run GoClip? +
Windows 10 or 11, an internet connection, and a TikTok account. Everything else — Python, ffmpeg, Whisper AI — is bundled inside the installer. No technical setup required.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account with no fees or penalties. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period.
